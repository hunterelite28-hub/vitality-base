Vitality · today's changes

What changed, and where to look

One card per change. Each tells you where to tap and what to look for. Read one in five seconds.

Fixed Nicer New
01

The stray light is gone Fixed

Where
Open your dashboard. Look at the Library tile (the one labelled 00).
Look for
That bright dot welded to the top edge is gone. Now a soft orb drifts across the middle, alive like every other tile.
02

Tile pictures pop now Nicer

Where
Your dashboard. The faint line-art in the background of every tile.
Look for
The art used to be washed-out and samey. The lines are brighter with a soft glow now, so each design stands on its own.
03

Library corner smudge gone Fixed

Where
Tap the Library tile to open the big Library window. Look at its top-left corner.
Look for
No more faint green haze in the corner. Clean flat black, edge to edge.
04

Tile buttons look pro Nicer

Where
Tap Customize. Look at the corner of any tile (the size / pencil / remove buttons).
Look for
Three floating buttons are now one neat little pill. Cleaner, real icons, and no lag when the tiles wiggle on your phone.
05

Design picker looks premium Nicer

Where
Tap Customize, then the pencil on a tile. The "Design your tile" pop-up.
Look for
The little design cards are centered and framed now, not flat and off to one side. Feels made, not thrown together.
06

Add a profile photo New

Where
Go to Account, then Public profile. Tap Add a photo. (Claim your handle first if you have not.)
Look for
Your photo shows top-right on the dashboard and on your public page. This one is already live on the real site, not just the preview.
07

Tile graphics are strong now Nicer

Where
Your dashboard. The line-art on the main tiles, especially Brand (the star shape) and Peak (the ring).
Look for
They used to fade into the tile and look dead. Now they are bright and confident with a soft glow, so each tile's graphic actually shows.
08

Dragging feels like iOS Nicer

Where
Tap Customize, then press and hold a tile and start moving it.
Look for
The rest of the board dims and the wobble stops, so the tile in your hand floats above and grows a touch. Now you can actually see the others slide to make room for where it will land.
